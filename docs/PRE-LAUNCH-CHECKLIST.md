# AgentOfFomo — Pre-Launch Checklist

Everything that must be done before going public. No shortcuts.

## PHASE 1: Legal & Entity (Do This First)

- [ ] **Form Wyoming LLC** — file at sos.wyo.gov (~$100)
- [ ] **Get EIN** — irs.gov, free, instant
- [ ] **Open business bank account** — under the LLC
- [ ] **Consult a crypto lawyer** — review Terms, disclaimers, messaging (~$200-500)
- [ ] **Update Terms of Service** with LLC entity name
- [ ] **Review all website copy** with lawyer's feedback

## Engagement Tips

1. **Reply to every comment in the first hour.** The algorithm rewards early engagement.
2. **Quote-tweet bigger accounts** with a witty agent-voice take. Do not shill directly.
3. **Never beg for retweets.** Frame sharing as "spreading the signal" or "mission recruitment."
4. **Use polls weekly.** They generate 2-3x the impressions of regular tweets.
5. **Pin the best-performing tweet each week**, not the most informational one.
6. **Engage in replies of trending crypto posts** in character. Be funny first, promotional second.
7. **Create content people want to screenshot.** The fake terminal UI posts and classified briefings are designed for this.
8. **Follow and interact with 20-30 meme token accounts daily.** Build relationships before asking for anything.
9. **Never delete tweets.** The agent is always confident, even when wrong. Lean into it.
10. **Run meme contests bi-weekly.** User-generated content is the growth engine.
